# Administrative Policies and Travel Information

## Travel and Expense Policies

### Business Travel
- Pre-approval required for all business travel
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Business class for flights over 6 hours
- Hotel booking through approved travel agency

### Travel Expenses
- Airfare: Actual cost (pre-approved)
- Hotel: Maximum $250/night for domestic, $350/night for international
- Meals: $50/day for domestic, $75/day for international
- Transportation: Actual cost with receipts

### Expense Reimbursement
- Submit expenses within 30 days of travel
- All receipts must be attached
- Approval required from department head
- Reimbursement processed within 10 business days

## Office Administration

### Office Supplies
- Standard supplies available through internal catalog
- Special requests require manager approval
- Monthly budget allocation per department
- Eco-friendly options preferred

### Facility Management
- Office maintenance requests via facility portal
- Emergency repairs: 24/7 response
- Regular cleaning schedule: Monday-Friday
- Security system monitored 24/7

### Vendor Management
- All vendors must be pre-approved
- Contract review by legal department
- Regular vendor performance reviews
- Preferred vendor program available

## Event Management

### Company Events
- Event planning requires 6-week advance notice
- Budget approval from senior management
- Venue selection through approved list
- Catering through preferred vendors

### Meeting Rooms
- Room booking through calendar system
- Maximum booking: 4 hours per day
- Audio-visual equipment available
- Catering coordination available

## Administrative Procedures

### Purchase Orders
- All purchases over $500 require PO
- Three-quote requirement for purchases over $10,000
- Approval hierarchy based on amount
- Electronic approval system

### Invoice Processing
- Invoices processed within 15 days
- Three-way match: PO, receipt, invoice
- Electronic invoice preferred
- Payment terms: Net 30
