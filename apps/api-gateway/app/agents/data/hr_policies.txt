# HR Policies and Leave Information

## Leave Policies

### Annual Leave
- Employees are entitled to 25 days of annual leave per year
- Leave must be approved by immediate supervisor
- Minimum notice period: 2 weeks for leave longer than 5 days
- Maximum consecutive leave: 15 days without special approval

### Sick Leave
- 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- Medical certificate required for absences over 3 days
- Emergency sick leave can be taken without prior notice

### Maternity Leave
- 12 weeks of paid maternity leave
- 6 weeks before expected delivery date
- 6 weeks after delivery
- Extended unpaid leave available up to 6 months

### Paternity Leave
- 2 weeks of paid paternity leave
- Must be taken within 8 weeks of birth
- Can be taken in one continuous period or split

### Public Holidays
- 12 public holidays per year
- Additional regional holidays may apply
- Holiday pay is 100% of basic salary

## HR Procedures

### Leave Application Process
1. Submit leave request via HR portal
2. Get supervisor approval
3. HR department processes request
4. Confirmation sent via email

### Performance Reviews
- Quarterly performance reviews
- Annual performance appraisals
- 360-degree feedback system
- Salary adjustments based on performance

### Employee Benefits
- Health insurance for employee and family
- Dental coverage
- Life insurance
- Retirement savings plan
- Professional development allowance
